Syrian Battle

Top Kek Gaming's explosive free-to-play FPS is now available for download. Compelling Co-op and fast-paced action are waiting for you every day.

Co-op unite and conquer

Join forces with friends to annihilate the enemy. In Syrian Battle's unique Co-op mode you can play with up to five people to take down Blackwood forces around the globe. With new challenges and rewards every day, players of all levels will find something worth fighting for!

  1. New player experience

    Play tutorial missions and master every class. First, learn the craft of Rifleman and Sniper, and then get to grips with the support abilities of Engineer and Medic. You'll also discover an easy-to-access co-op experience, where players can choose the difficulty they want to face.

  2. teamwork

    Co-op success depends on teamwork and the combination of multiple soldier classes. Hone your skills and earn rewards for collaboration and individual heroics, such as lifting teammates to higher ground, providing them with ammo, or pushing heavy obstacles together.

  3. Co-op reward system

    Soldiers that complete co-op missions earn crowns for their efforts. Team performance is rated in several categories and each teammate is rewarded accordingly. Brave soldiers can unlock new rewards by replaying missions and improving their record. There truly is no 'i' in team; you win or lose together.

Versus state your superiority

Terrorists and US army go up against each other in intense Versus match-ups. And with up to eight players on each side, the action always comes thick and fast! You can swap roles in-game at any time, maximizing your performance on the battlefield.

  1. Team Death Match

    Team to team showdown! Teams can claim victory by reaching the maximum score or earning more points than the opposition before time runs out. This classic mode allows for individual initiative whilst still rewarding teamwork.

  2. Storm

    This assault mode puts one team on the offensive as they aim to capture three strategic points in succession. The other team has to defend their ground, and sides are switched when the time expires or all the points are taken. These battles are longer, suiting players who desire conquest and strategy.

  3. Free for all

    A true soldier must stand alone sometimes. In this fun and open mode, success comes down to quick thinking, adaptability and stealth. Whoever reaches the maximum score first or has the most points when the time expires is the winner

  4. Destruction

    In this mode, teams can achieve victory if they deliver three air strikes on enemy positions. To call an airstrike, one team must hold the control point located in the center of the map. If no team has entered a capture point within 120 seconds of the fight starting, the match is automatically ended in defeat.

  5. plant the bomb

    Teamwork is the key to detonation. One team tries to plant a bomb in the enemy base, while the other attempts to defuse it or wipe out the attacking forces. Try your hand at both roles and strive for planting perfection!

  6. tactics

    Experiment and adapt your play to meet the situation at hand - switching classes during the game and instantly bringing something new to the battle. Take advantage of your surroundings and strike from unexpected directions by mastering the moves and special actions, such as slide kills or flanking opponents.

Classes play your part

Syrian Battle offers players four soldier classes with different tactical approaches and advantages: Rifleman, Sniper, Medic, and Engineer.

  1. Lead the line into critical areas and overwhelm the enemy by providing a constant flow of ammo to your teammates.

    rifleman

    An all-round role providing the firepower to drive their team forward. The Rifleman can resupply ammo to himself and his teammates. They are best suited for close- to medium-range combat.

    Starting Gear:

    Assault Rifle R4A1

    Special Actions:

    Resupply ammunition

  2. Use claymores in front of doors or on street corners for an element of surprise. help your teammates survive longer by restoring their armor.

    engineer

    The Engineer is a support class who can restore both their own and their teammates' armor. Engineers can also lay claymores and detonate explosives faster than other classes. The Engineer is best suited for close-to medium-range combat.

    Starting Gear:

    Submachine Gun Z84 MG Special

    Special Actions:

    Restore armor and plant claymores

  3. Heal your teammates to provide endurance against strong enemies. defibrillators can also serve a deadly double purpose.

    medic

    Being the traditional 'recovery' class, Medics can heal both themselves and their teammates, and can revive allies who have fallen. Their standard weapon is best suited for close-range combat.

    Starting Gear:

    Richmond 770 Shotgun

    Special Actions:

    Heal and revive allies

  4. Find a vantage point and pick off your enemies from the shadows. Just make sure you keep moving to avoid detection.

    sniper

    Snipers provide long-range support and are deadly accurate. Even though they don't have the additional skills of the other classes, their advantages from distance and highest damage ratio per bullet make them essential to any team.

    Starting Gear:

    Sniper Rifle SVK

    Special Actions:

    Take down critical targets from afar

features

  1. 3D Engine-powered graphics

    Syrian Battle features intuitive controls and slick gameplay thanks to the power of Crytek's state-of-the-art CRYENGINE. Immersive play is ensured with high quality graphics and the game runs great on low-spec PCs while also scaling for high-end hardware

  2. Gameplay actions

    Syrian Battle offers distinctive all-classes abilities which include vaulting obstacles and planting explosives. Players can also slide, allowing for new offensive and defensive play techniques such as XP-boosting slide kills.

  3. Unique team-support actions

    Help allies to higher ground and pull them to their feet when they're downed. Individual moves also make for fluid movement in battle as you leap obstacles, slide, and more.

  4. Real-time weapon support customization

    Adapt your weapons on the go to maximize efficiency on the battlefield. Tweak your gun to perfection with under barrel grenade launchers, advanced scopes, deadly silencers and more!

Locations

A world struggling to emerge from decades of devastation, battered and beaten by the deepest and longest economic freefall in modern history – this recession has affected the strongest national identities and moral ideals.A gradually smaller percentage of powerful individuals control an increasingly greater percentage of the global wealth. Against threats to its ever more comprehensive interests, 'Late Capitalism' fortifies its ivory tower with walled compounds, weapon systems and the unregulated might of Blackwood.

Fight in the Syria; Counter terrorists's expansion into the cities; take to the favelas of Syria to expose their inhuman experiments; and prevent Syrian exploitation of the oil rich regions there.

new world order consipancy, corruption, and chaos

  1. Us army

    US General Lee Warton was both respected and feared by his men for his fierce determination in combat - earning him the nickname 'S.B.'. Warton has come out of retirement to stand against the corruption and violent profiteering of Blackwood. Hundreds join his rapidly growing movement, named after the general himself. Syrian Battle deploys small teams of experienced and highly specialized infantry operators to strike against Blackwood.

  2. Terrorists

    Powerful individuals own the majority of the world's wealth. They assert their will and protect their money through the power of an army unit known as SyrBat – a collection of smaller military forces forged into an effective and ruthless fighting machine by its CEO, Ursula Ellis. Blackwood believes in strength in numbers and supports their troops with mechanized assets - tanks, assault helicopters, and combat mechs.
